Medicine Unbridled

Veterinarians and Multispecies Statecraft in the Enlightenment

Chapters:

  • First there were horses
  • Then cattle
  • And sheep
  • Next pigs
  • Also cats
  • And dogs
  • But not quite chickens

There were, of course, also

  • Wolves
  • Bacillus anthracis
  • Rats
  • Deer
  • Worms
  • Goats
  • Asses
This work recasts the Enlightenment as a multispecies affair, one where new visions of statecraft saw the governance of nonhuman animals as essential in governing humans.
